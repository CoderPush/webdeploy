Who we are#

CoderPush is a remote first team of engineers and designers who collaborate with startups to build great software, teams, and processes.

We set a very high expectation of client satisfaction for the long term. Some of our clients have volunteered their time to leave testimonials on Clutch.

What you’ll work on#

If hired, one immediate product to work on to is Sleek “the technology solution that makes the lives of entrepreneurs and investors easier”

Joining this project you will work directly in a team that span across Vietnam, The Philippines and Singapore.

We are looking for someone with#

  • Good communication skills in English.
  • 5 years experience working as a QA Engineer, including at least 2 years working as an Automation QA Engineer
  • Confident with Selenium Grid
  • Familiar with Allure-Behave is a plus
  • Humility to learn what you don’t know, every day.
  • Ability to understand and define all actions to be performed when assigned to a task.
  • Ability to manage time when facing difficulties or under pressure or deadlines.

Benefits#

  • Full-time insurances and benefits.
  • Salary $1.5k-3.5k gross depending on experience.
  • Performance-based bonuses tied to project deliverables.
  • Sponsorships to take part in sports and tech activities.

Office#

We are remote-first, which means working remotely does not make you feel less important or bored. However, people who love office atmosphere still can still enjoy going there. Our humble place is in the beautiful City Garden in Binh Thanh district. We hold weekly video and board games every Friday from 4 pm till late – a great time to host friends, clients, and job applicants!

Personal growth#

Still here? You must be thinking about how you’ll grow. That’s one of our core values.

Why join a young company instead of a big one? Because you can learn faster, make mistakes, share learning from mistakes, and have a personal impact on those around you. You shape how the company forms.

Why a software studio instead of a product company? It’s like spending a few years at a training camp, for something that you love doing :) There should be more companies like ours in Vietnam, even South East Asia, but we don’t know many. And by the way, we build to last, without the pressure of investment capital.

Why CoderPush instead of just another firm? Because we build teams first, products second. We have been cofounders of a bilingual school, a coding school, a running club, and a few funded startups (let’s exchange stories!).

And lastly, join to build a company like building great code itself: continuous improvement, continuous delivery, refactoring, technical debt, and finally, contributing to open source.
